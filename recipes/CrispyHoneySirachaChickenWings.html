Crispy Honey Sriracha Chicken Wings

Description

Crispy Honey Sriracha Chicken Wings are a delicious and simple recipe that uses a unique baking powder-laced spice rub to achieve a crispy texture similar to fried wings. The wings are baked, turned twice, and glazed with a mix of honey, sriracha, rice vinegar, and sesame oil. A sprinkle of sesame seeds adds a nice finishing touch. This method creates a flavorful and crispy dish that's perfect for any occasion.

Nutrional Information

Calories : 315 Fat : 14g Carbs : 21g Protein : 19g

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 ½ tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 ½ tablespoons freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1 ¼ pounds chicken wing sections
    • Honey Siracha Glaze:
  • ⅙ cup honey
  • ⅙ cup sriracha sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon sesame oil
  • ½ pinch sesame seeds, or as desired

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and place an oven-p roof wire rack over the foil.
  2. Whisk baking powder, salt, black pepper, and paprika together in a small bowl.
  3. Place chicken wings in a large bowl. Sprinkle 1/2 of the baking powder mixture over wings and toss to coat. Repeat with the remaining baking powder mixture, tossing well to coat.
  4. Arrange wings on the rack of the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Turn wings and continue baking for 20 minutes. Turn wings again and bake until wings are browned and crispy, about 15 minutes more. Transfer wings to a large bowl.
  6. For the glaze: Whisk honey, sriracha sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil together in a bowl until smooth. Drizzle glaze over wings and toss to coat completely.
  7. Transfer wings to a serving platter and sprinkle sesame seeds over the top.
