Coffe Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description

These delightful cookies combine the comforting flavors of coffee and chocolate. The subtle coffee notes enhance the richness of the chocolate chips, creating a perfectly balanced treat. They're soft, chewy, and ideal for a morning indulgence or an afternoon pick-me-up. Enjoy a warm, satisfying bite of these unique cookies!

Nutrional Information

Calories : 112 Fat : 5g Carbs : 16g Protein : 2g

Ingredients

  • ⅜ cup blanched slivered almonds
  • ⅙ cup instant coffee granules
  • 1 tablespoon hot water
  • 1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • ⅜ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅝ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup butter, softened
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Toast almonds in a single layer on a baking sheet in the preheated oven until brown, about 10 minutes.
  3. Dissolve coffee in 2 tablespoons hot water in a small bowl; set aside.
  4. Whisk flour, baking soda, and salt together in a separate bowl; set aside.
  5. Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer at a medium speed until fluffy.
  6. Add coffee, eggs, and vanilla extract; mix well.
  7. Reduce mixer's speed to low; slowly add flour mixture.
  8. Fold chocolate chips and almonds into dough.
  9. Drop teaspoonfuls of dough 3 inches apart onto an ungreased cookie sheet.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes.
